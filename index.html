The Problem

Finding a cake that's actually eggless shouldn't take all week.

Most "eggless" cakes are a supermarket cake with the egg quietly left out — dense, dry, and baked in the same kitchen as everything else. Genuinely egg-free usually means a long drive across Sydney, or a home baker who can't guarantee your date. And when the party's in five days, most places just say no.

We built Num Num's around one fix: send us a photo, we recreate it 100% eggless, fresh in 48 hours. Every flavour, every size, every day — not a special order.

Good to Know

Eggless Cake Questions, Answered

Are your cakes really 100% eggless?
Yes. Every cake we bake is completely egg-free — not just the ones labelled eggless. There are no eggs anywhere in our kitchen, so vegetarian, Jain-friendly and halal-friendly families can order anything on the menu without asking twice.
Can you make a cake from a photo I send?
Yes. Send us a photo from Instagram, Pinterest or anywhere else and we’ll recreate the design for your occasion. Our gallery is a starting point, not a limit — if you can show us a picture, we can make the cake.
How much notice do you need?
Custom cakes need at least 48 hours’ notice. Message us your date, design and pickup store, and we’ll confirm your order the same day.
Do you deliver, or is it pickup?
Every order is made fresh for pickup from our Harris Park or Riverstone stores, ready at the time you choose.
How do I get a quote?
Message us on WhatsApp or call with your date, design and pickup store. Quotes are free and there’s no obligation to order.
