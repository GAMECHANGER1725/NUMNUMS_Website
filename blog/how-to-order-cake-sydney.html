    Message +61 425 697 725 with: event date, servings needed, flavour, decoration notes, and which location you'll collect from (Harris Park or Riverstone). You can also use the online form at numnumsbakery.com.au/order.
  5. 5
    Confirm and collect
    We reply within a few hours (during business hours) with confirmation, price, and pick-up time. Pay on collection (or deposit for complex orders). Collect at Harris Park (96/96 Wigram Street, daily 11 am–10 pm) or Riverstone (Shop 8, Riverstone Shopping Centre, Mon–Fri 6 am–8 pm, Sat–Sun 7 am–7 pm).

How Much Notice Do You Actually Need?

The stated minimum is 48 hours. In practice, this means placing your order by the same time, two days before your event. If your party is on Saturday afternoon, you need to have sent your WhatsApp message by Thursday afternoon at the latest. But "minimum" is not the same as "comfortable" — here is what different lead times realistically allow:

  • 48 hours: sufficient for a standard single-tier cake in a readily available flavour with a simple message. No complex decoration, no special requests. Best suited for informal occasions where perfection is nice but not essential.
  • 3–4 days: comfortable for single or double-tier cakes with buttercream decoration, a specific colour scheme, or a custom message in a particular font or style. This is the sweet spot for most milestone birthday cakes.
  • 5–7 days: recommended for complex fondant work, 3D sculpted elements, fresh florals, edible printing, or multi-tier architectural cakes. The decoration on these cakes is applied in stages and requires set time between layers.
  • 7–14 days (December only): November and December are Num Num's busiest period — Christmas, New Year, school end-of-year events, and holiday parties all compress into 6 weeks. Slots during this period fill faster than any other time of year. If your event is in December, order as soon as you know the date.

What Happens After You Send Your WhatsApp Message?

The response cycle at Num Num's is managed by a human team member, not an automated chatbot. During business hours (Harris Park: 11 am–10 pm daily; Riverstone: 6 am–8 pm Mon–Fri), you can typically expect a reply within 2–4 hours. Messages sent outside business hours are responded to at opening the following day.

The confirmation message will include:

  • Confirmation that your requested date and flavour are available
  • A price for your requested size and decoration
  • Your confirmed pick-up time
  • Any questions the team has about your decoration brief (usually only needed for complex designs)
  • A deposit request, if applicable (complex or large orders)

Once you confirm the order and pay any deposit, your slot is secured. All custom orders are made fresh, beginning the day before or on the morning of collection depending on size. This means your cake has not been sitting in a display case — it is made for you, for your event, on the scheduled date.

Are There Restrictions on What Can Be Custom-Ordered?

The short answer is: very few. Num Num's Bakery can accommodate most standard custom requests. The constraints that do exist are practical rather than arbitrary:

  • Nut-free decoration: Pistachio-based decoration cannot be offered as "completely nut-free" because the same workspace handles pistachio products. If a guest has a severe tree nut allergy, choose a flavour and decoration that does not include pistachio, and mention the allergy when ordering so the team can advise on cross-contact risk for your specific choice.
  • Alcohol-based flavours: Num Num's does not produce rum, amaretto, or alcohol-infused cake flavours. All products are alcohol-free by default.
  • Eggs: Not available. Every product is egg-free. If you specifically need an egg-containing cake, Num Num's is not the right bakery — and that is a feature, not a limitation, for the vast majority of customers who choose Num Num's precisely because of the eggless guarantee.
  • Same-day orders: Not available as a standard service. Last-minute availability (less than 48 hours) does occasionally arise from cancellations — WhatsApp to ask, but it cannot be relied upon for important occasions.
