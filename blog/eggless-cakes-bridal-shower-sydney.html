Why Are Eggless Bridal Shower Cakes the Smarter Choice in Sydney?

According to the Australian Bureau of Statistics, approximately 83,000 marriages were registered in Australia in 2023 (ABS, Marriages and Divorces, Australia, 2023). Behind most of those weddings sat a bridal shower — and behind most of those showers sat the question of what cake to order for a guest list that almost certainly includes people with dietary restrictions.

Egg allergy is one of the most common food allergies in Australia. According to Allergy & Anaphylaxis Australia, approximately 9% of Australian infants have an egg allergy — a figure that translates into a remarkable number of families at any large celebration. And the broader picture of allergic disease in Australia has changed dramatically over the past two decades. In August 2025, the National Allergy Centre of Excellence reported that 8.2 million Australians — 30% of the population — now live with allergic disease, up from just 4.1 million (19.6%) in 2007. That's the population of an entire country, doubled in under two decades.

Beyond allergy, Sydney's multicultural guest lists bring a whole range of dietary preferences that make an eggless cake the genuinely smarter, more inclusive default. Hindu vegetarian guests exclude eggs from their diet. Jain dietary practice excludes eggs. Some Muslim families prefer to know exactly what's in their food. When your bridal shower cake is eggless across the board, none of these conversations need to happen at the dessert table.

Which Flavours Work Best for a Bridal Shower Cake?

The best bridal shower cake flavour is the one your guests will actually want seconds of — and with 15 options on our menu, there's a clear answer for almost every celebration style. From our order history, bridal shower orders at Num Num's cluster strongly around a handful of flavours, though the right choice really depends on your theme, your guest list, and how formal or relaxed you want the afternoon to feel.

White Forest is our most consistently requested bridal shower flavour. It's elegant, light, and visually beautiful — a cream-based cake with cherry accents that photographs beautifully against a pastel table. Strawberry is close behind: feminine and pink-hued, with a freshness that suits afternoon events perfectly. If your shower has a romantic or whimsical aesthetic, these two are the obvious starting points.

Lychee has become a standout option for multicultural celebrations — particularly for South Asian and East Asian guests who appreciate its delicate, fragrant quality. It's the flavour we get the most surprised positive reactions about from guests who'd never tried it before. Vanilla, meanwhile, remains the reliable crowd-pleaser: it pairs beautifully with buttercream florals and gives the decorator maximum canvas to work with.

Butterscotch tends to surprise people at bridal showers. It's warm and nostalgic, and guests who might not be drawn to fruit flavours often find it's their favourite slice. Rasmalai is the top pick for celebrations where South Asian flavours are woven into the menu and the occasion — fragrant, milk-based, and distinctive in the best possible way.

For premium gatherings with a more adult focus, Ferrero Rocher (rich hazelnut chocolate, always a crowd-stopper) and Tiramisu (coffee-forward, sophisticated) are consistently popular. These aren't casual birthday flavours — they're genuinely special-occasion choices. Browse the full selection on our Our Cakes page.

What Sizes and Tiers Can You Order for a Bridal Shower?

Getting the size right is one of the most practical parts of ordering a bridal shower cake — and it's something we help customers work through every time via WhatsApp. The short guide: know your guest count, decide whether the cake is the centrepiece or one of several desserts, and factor in whether guests are likely to have seconds.

  • 6-inch round — serves 8–10 guests. Perfect for intimate showers with a close circle of family or friends.
  • 8-inch round — serves 12–16 guests. The most popular size for a typical bridal shower of 15–20 people where the cake is the hero.
  • 10-inch round — serves 20–25 guests. Right-sized for medium gatherings; allows for a generous slice per person without waste.
  • 12-inch round — serves 30–35 guests. For larger events, or when you know your crowd always goes back for more.
  • 2-tier custom — visually spectacular for 25–40 guests; allows two flavours and gives designers more to work with.
  • 3-tier custom — the full statement piece; suited to larger showers of 40+ or celebrations where the cake is the centrepiece of a styled shoot or photo gallery.

From our bridal shower order history, the 8-inch single-tier and 2-tier combination are the most common choices. A 2-tier bridal shower cake — particularly one with floral buttercream and a contrasting flavour per tier — has become increasingly popular as the "something to remember" centrepiece that guests photograph and share. If budget allows for it, the 2-tier is usually the choice people don't regret.

What About Allergen Transparency at Num Num's Bakery?

Food allergen transparency matters — particularly at events like bridal showers where you're catering for guests whose dietary needs you may not know completely. Under Australian food standards, FSANZ priority allergens include eggs, dairy (milk), tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, soy, fish, and shellfish. Serious allergic reactions to any of these can be severe, and the labelling requirements exist for very good reason.

At Num Num's Bakery, our position on eggs is categorical: they're not used anywhere. Not in any product, not in any preparation area, not on any equipment. This isn't a "we try to keep them separate" situation — eggs genuinely aren't present in our kitchen. That's what 100% eggless means. For guests managing egg allergy, there's no cross-contamination risk to navigate.

What we do use: dairy. Butter and milk are core ingredients across our entire range. Our cakes are not vegan. If you have guests who are dairy-free or fully plant-based, please let us know when you order — we'll give you a complete ingredient rundown for your chosen flavour and you can make an informed decision. We'd rather be completely upfront than have anyone feel uncertain at the event.

For any specific allergen queries beyond eggs, message us directly via WhatsApp at +61 425 697 725 before placing your order. We can provide a full ingredient breakdown by flavour on request. There are no hidden ingredients, and there's no reason to guess.
