How Do You Order an Eggless Cake Online?

You order an eggless cake online by sending one WhatsApp message — it takes under five minutes, and we reply to confirm before any payment. There's no separate "eggless" option to find, because the entire menu is already egg-free. The four steps below are all there is to it.

  1. Browse the flavours: Open the Our Cakes page and shortlist two or three of the 15 eggless flavours.
  2. Message us on WhatsApp: Send your order to +61 425 697 725 with flavour, size, pick-up date and any design idea.
  3. Confirm and pay a deposit: We reply with availability and a price, confirm the details in writing, and take a deposit to lock it in.
  4. Collect: Pick up from Harris Park or Riverstone at your chosen time — Num Num's is currently pick-up only.

Prefer a longer walkthrough with photos? Our companion guide on how to order a custom eggless cake online covers the design side in more detail, and the Order Online page has the full process and sizing chart.

What Information Should You Include in Your Online Order?

The more detail you send in your first message, the faster we can confirm and quote — most back-and-forth happens when a key detail is missing. From our order data, the messages we turn around quickest include all six of the items below up front. Treat it as a simple checklist before you hit send.

  • Flavour — one of the 15 eggless options (or a top-two if you'd like a recommendation).
  • Size or guest count — tell us how many people, and we'll suggest the right size.
  • Pick-up date and time — and your chosen shop, Harris Park or Riverstone.
  • Design or message — a theme, colours, or the writing you want on top.
  • Dietary notes — egg-free is standard; flag any other allergens so we can advise.
  • A reference photo — optional, but a picture of a style you like helps a lot.

From our own order history, the smoothest online orders are the ones where the customer sends a clear photo plus a guest number in the first message. We can quote on the spot, suggest a size, and reserve the date in one reply — no five-message thread, no missed details. It's a small habit that saves everyone time, especially in a busy festival week.

How Much Notice Does an Online Eggless Cake Order Need?

Give at least 48 hours for a standard eggless cake, and 5–7 days for a custom or tiered design — every cake is baked fresh to order, so there's no shelf stock to pull from. From our internal order data, the requests we most often can't fulfil are same-day custom designs during a busy weekend, simply because the kitchen is already committed.

Notice matters even more around festivals. Diwali, Eid, Christmas and the NSW school-holiday weeks are our busiest stretches, and popular pick-up dates book out early. If your event lands in one of those windows, message us a week or two ahead to be safe rather than sorry.

How Do You Choose the Right Cake Size When Ordering Online?

Match the size to your guest count, not the photo on screen — online, it's easy to under-order. From our orders, a 6-inch round serves roughly 8–10 people, an 8-inch covers 15–20, and a 10-inch round stretches to 30–40. If you're between sizes, round up; a few extra slices rarely go to waste.

For bigger numbers, a two-tier cake or two separate flavours often beats one oversized single tier — it's easier to cut and lets you offer a choice. When you message us your guest count, we'll recommend a size and, if it helps, a flavour split before you commit.

Is Ordering an Eggless Cake Online Safe for Allergies?

For egg allergy, yes — our entire kitchen is egg-free, which removes the egg cross-contamination pathway. According to Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ), eggs are one of Australia's priority food allergens, and an entirely egg-free environment is a meaningful safeguard that a general bakery using eggs elsewhere can't match.

An honest caveat: egg-free is not allergen-free. Our cakes contain dairy (milk powder, butter), may involve nuts, and standard cakes contain wheat. For a severe or anaphylactic allergy, state your needs clearly when you order online and follow your own medical or Allergy & Anaphylaxis Australia-aligned advice. We'll provide a full ingredient breakdown for your chosen flavour — see our dedicated guide to eggless cake for egg allergies for more detail.

What Makes Ordering From a Dedicated Eggless Bakery Different?

The difference is that the cake was designed egg-free from its very first recipe test — not adapted on the day from a standard sponge. A general bakery offering "an eggless option" is usually swapping eggs out of a recipe built around them, which is why those cakes so often come out dense or dry. Every Num Num's recipe was developed without eggs from the ground up, so the texture is consistent order after order.

That consistency is the reason online customers come back. From our internal order data, repeat customers most often cite the same thing: the cake tastes and looks like a normal cake, every single time, with no nasty surprise on the day of the event. When you're ordering sight-unseen online, that predictability matters more than anything — you're trusting us before you've seen the cake.

There's a safety dimension too. Because the whole kitchen is egg-free, there's no shared equipment or prep surface where egg could sneak in.
