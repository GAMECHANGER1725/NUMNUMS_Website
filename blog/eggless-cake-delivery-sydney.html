Does Eggless Cake Delivery Actually Work — or Does Texture Suffer?

The most common concern about ordering an eggless cake for delivery is texture: will it arrive dry, crumbly, or collapsed? The short answer is no — if the bakery knows what it's doing. Eggs serve four roles in conventional baking: emulsification, leavening, structure, and moisture retention. Num Num's Bakery has been replacing all four functions with precision-tested combinations since 2019, and our cakes travel just as well as their egg-containing counterparts.

The key lies in what we use as replacements. Flaxseed meal plus water creates a binding gel that replicates the structural protein network eggs form. Aquafaba (the liquid from cooked chickpeas) contributes foam and emulsification. A careful ratio of baking powder and bicarbonate of soda handles leavening. And a slightly higher butter-to-flour ratio compensates for moisture. The result is a cake with a tight, even crumb that stays moist for 2–3 days after baking — more than enough time for delivery, celebration, and leftovers.

Is Eggless Cake Delivery Safe for Egg Allergy Households?

Food allergy safety in baked goods is a serious concern. Australia has the second-highest rate of food allergy hospitalisation in the world, and eggs are among the top eight allergens regulated under the Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ) Code Standard 1.2.3. Num Num's Bakery's approach to allergen management goes beyond simply omitting eggs from recipes.

Our kitchen is a dedicated eggless facility. No eggs have ever been brought into our production environment. This eliminates the cross-contamination risk that affects households ordering from mainstream bakeries with shared production lines. Every ingredient we use is sourced from suppliers that can confirm egg-free supply chains for the products we order. Staff training reinforces allergen awareness at every step of production, packaging, and delivery.

That said, if you have a severe egg allergy and require written confirmation of our allergen controls before ordering, please ask via WhatsApp and we'll send you our ingredient documentation. We want your family to celebrate with complete confidence.

How Do Eggless Cakes Compare to Regular Cakes on Delivery Durability?

A 2022 study published in the Journal of Food Science found that moisture retention in oil-based eggless sponge cakes was comparable to egg-based counterparts over a 72-hour refrigerated window, with the eggless variants showing slightly lower staling rates due to higher fat-to-protein ratios. In practical terms: our eggless cakes hold up extremely well during delivery, and here's why.

We package every delivery cake in a custom-fit cardboard box with a non-slip base liner. Tiered cakes are secured with internal dowels before boxing. Fondant or mirror-glaze finishing is applied after final chilling to minimise surface cracking during transit. We also recommend the following to customers receiving a delivery:

  • Keep the box flat and level during transport from your front door to the fridge.
  • Do not refrigerate cakes with fresh cream decorations for longer than 24 hours — allow to come to room temperature 20–30 minutes before serving for best texture.
  • Buttercream-finished cakes can be stored at room temperature (below 22°C) for up to 2 days.
  • For outdoor events or hot weather, request a fondant or ganache finish for extra stability.
