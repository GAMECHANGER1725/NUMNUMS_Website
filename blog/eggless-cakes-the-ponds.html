The Ponds: One of Sydney's Most Vibrant Communities

The Ponds is more than just a suburb — it's a statement about how the north-western corridor has changed. What was once farmland is now home to sports clubs, community centres, well-regarded schools, and thousands of families who chose the area for its green spaces and newer housing stock.

A significant proportion of residents have South Asian, Sri Lankan, Chinese, and Middle Eastern backgrounds. Many follow dietary practices that exclude eggs — Hindu vegetarianism, Jain guidelines, or personal health choices. For these families, the question of where to buy a genuinely eggless cake for a birthday, naming ceremony, Diwali celebration, or school event is not trivial. Standard supermarkets don't offer it. Most bakeries treat it as an afterthought.

Num Num's Bakery was built to be the answer to that gap. Every product we make — across all 15 flavours, all sizes, all occasions — is 100% eggless. Not a special order. Not a modified recipe. It is simply how we bake.

One Cake That Works for Every Guest

One of the most common scenarios we hear from The Ponds families: a child's birthday party with 20 to 30 guests. Some follow vegetarian diets. Some keep Jain principles. Some are simply egg-intolerant or have personal preferences. In a typical party setting, this creates a planning headache — do you buy two cakes? Do you ask the bakery about ingredients? Do you trust the substitution?

With Num Num's, that problem disappears entirely. One cake serves the entire table. Every guest can eat every slice. The host doesn't need to manage separate food options or explain ingredient choices. That simplicity is genuinely valuable — and it's one of the main reasons The Ponds families keep coming back to us for every celebration.

Getting Here from The Ponds

From The Ponds, take Schofields Road west to connect with Richmond Road, then head north into Riverstone. The Riverstone Shopping Centre is on Market Street in the heart of the Riverstone village. Total drive time is typically 10–15 minutes from most addresses in The Ponds, depending on traffic.
