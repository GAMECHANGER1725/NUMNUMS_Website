Eggless Cakes in Tallawong: Order Fresh from Riverstone — 8 Minutes Away

1 June 2026 7 min read Location · Eggless Cakes

Tallawong is one of Sydney's newest suburbs — a residential precinct that has grown up around the Sydney Metro Northwest's Tallawong Station, the northern terminus of the line. First home deliveries in Tallawong began around 2020, and the suburb has been filling in steadily since, with new families arriving from across Sydney's western and south-western suburbs.

As a brand-new suburb, Tallawong has a well-known limitation: the local service infrastructure is still developing. There are no dedicated cake bakeries in Tallawong itself. For residents who need a custom, freshly made eggless cake for a birthday, naming ceremony, baby shower, or cultural celebration, the closest proper option is Num Num's Bakery at Riverstone Shopping Centre — just 8–12 minutes away by car.

Quick Summary
  • Num Num's Bakery at Riverstone is 8–12 min from Tallawong by car
  • Also accessible by train: Tallawong Station (Metro NW) → Riverstone Station
  • Located at Shop 8, Riverstone Shopping Centre — Mon–Fri 6 am–8 pm, Sat–Sun 7 am–7 pm
  • 100% eggless — all 15 flavours, every size, no exceptions
  • Order via WhatsApp — 48 hours notice — pick up fresh from Riverstone

Tallawong: The Metro Suburb That Needed a Bakery

Tallawong occupies a unique position in Sydney's geography. It is the northern terminus of the Sydney Metro Northwest — a major piece of public transport infrastructure that connects the corridor through Rouse Hill, Kellyville, Castle Hill, and ultimately to the CBD. The station was designed as a catalyst for residential development, and that development has arrived on schedule.

The community that has moved to Tallawong is diverse, energetic, and young — many households are first-time home buyers or families with young children who chose the suburb for its new infrastructure and transport links. A significant portion of residents have South Asian, Sri Lankan, and broader multicultural backgrounds where dietary restrictions around eggs are part of everyday life, not an occasional consideration.

For these families, finding an eggless cake for a child's first birthday, a Diwali celebration, or a baby shower used to mean a long drive to Parramatta or Harris Park. The Riverstone location of Num Num's Bakery has changed that. It is the closest proper cake bakery — accessible by car in under 12 minutes, and uniquely, also accessible by public transport.

Getting Here by Car

From Tallawong, head south along Hollinsworth Road, which connects to Richmond Road heading south into Riverstone. The Riverstone Shopping Centre is in the heart of Riverstone village on Market Street. Google Maps typically shows 8–12 minutes from most Tallawong streets, depending on the specific address and time of day.

Getting Here by Train

Tallawong is one of the few suburbs in north-western Sydney where the bakery is also accessible by public transport. Take the Sydney Metro Northwest from Tallawong Station to Riverstone Station. From Riverstone Station, the Shopping Centre is a short, walkable distance — under 10 minutes on foot, less if you take a quick rideshare. For residents without a car, or on days when parking seems inconvenient, the train option makes Num Num's Bakery genuinely accessible in a way that no other cake shop in the area can claim.

Why Eggless Cakes Matter in Tallawong

Tallawong's newcomers come from all parts of Sydney, but many have roots in communities where eggless food is standard — not a dietary preference, but a lifestyle. Hindu families following vegetarian principles, Jain families with strict dietary codes, and families where eggs are avoided for cultural, health, or personal reasons all need a cake option that works unconditionally for every guest.

At Num Num's Bakery, that is not a niche service — it is the entire business. Every product we make is 100% eggless. Not one range, not one option, not a "vegan-friendly" line — everything, all the time. A guest with any of the dietary backgrounds described above can eat every slice of the cake at your celebration without any questions needed.
