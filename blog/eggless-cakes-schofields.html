Why Eggless Matters in Schofields

Schofields has one of the most diverse demographics in the north-western corridor. A large proportion of residents come from South Asian communities — Indian, Sri Lankan, Bangladeshi — where Hindu vegetarianism, Jain dietary practices, or personal preference means eggs are excluded from their diet.

For these families, a standard supermarket cake or conventional bakery presents an immediate problem: either accept a product containing eggs, or ask for a special substitution that many bakeries simply can't do well. The result is often a drier, denser cake that doesn't taste or look like everyone else's.

Num Num's Bakery was founded specifically to eliminate that compromise. Every cake we make — every single one, across all 15 flavours and every size — contains no eggs whatsoever. This isn't a special dietary line. It is the entire menu. When you order from us, every guest at your table can eat the same cake without any questions asked.
