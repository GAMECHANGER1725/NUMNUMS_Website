Are Eggless Cakes Right for Carlingford's Multicultural Community?

Egg avoidance is remarkably common across Carlingford's diverse communities, and for a range of different reasons. Understanding why eggless matters to different households helps explain why Num Num's Bakery has become the go-to cake shop for such a broad cross-section of suburban Sydney families.

For Hindu vegetarian households — which represent a significant portion of Carlingford's Indian community — eggs are excluded from the diet under lacto-vegetarian practice. In this tradition, dairy is permitted but eggs are classified as non-vegetarian. A standard cake from a conventional bakery cannot be placed on the table at a Hindu household celebration without awkward conversations or outright exclusion of guests. Our cakes eliminate that problem entirely.

For Buddhist households within Carlingford's substantial Chinese and Korean communities, dietary practices vary significantly by tradition and region of origin. Many Chinese Buddhist dietary codes exclude meat and eggs, particularly for older generations. A cake made entirely without eggs and with no meat-based products is a natural fit for these households' celebration needs.

For Jain households, which avoid all animal products including eggs, our menu is compatible on the egg dimension — and our team is always happy to discuss specific ingredient questions via WhatsApp before ordering.

For egg-allergic families — which, as noted above, represent approximately 9% of Australian infants — the critical distinction is not just the absence of eggs in the recipe, but the absence of eggs from the entire kitchen environment. According to Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ), cross-contamination from shared equipment, surfaces, and preparation areas is a serious risk for egg-allergic individuals. At Num Num's Bakery, no eggs are used anywhere on the premises — making the cross-contamination risk meaningfully lower than at bakeries that merely offer an eggless option alongside egg-containing products.

One important note for all customers: our cakes contain dairy — milk powder, butter, and cream are used in our recipes. We are an eggless bakery, not a vegan bakery. If you or your guests require dairy-free or fully allergen-free products, please contact us via WhatsApp before ordering so we can discuss your options and ensure we're the right fit for your needs.
