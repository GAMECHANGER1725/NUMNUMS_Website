Why Don't Bakeries Near Guildford Offer Proper Eggless Options?

The short answer is that genuine eggless baking is hard. Eggs serve three distinct roles in conventional cake recipes: they bind ingredients, add moisture, and provide leavening lift. Removing them isn't a one-ingredient swap — it's an engineering problem that requires either a combination of functional substitutes (aquafaba, flaxseed meal, apple cider vinegar, soda bicarbonate in precise ratios) or a completely reformulated recipe built from scratch without eggs as an assumption.

Most Guildford and Merrylands bakeries don't invest in that reformulation because eggless isn't their primary market — it's a request they accommodate when asked. The result is often a denser, slightly gummy product that works adequately but doesn't match the texture of a well-made conventional cake.

Num Num's Bakery was built the other way around. The entire menu — all 15 flavours, every size, every custom design — was developed without eggs from day one. The team never learned to bake with eggs and then substituted them out; they built the recipes to be eggless from the ground up. That difference shows in the final product.

According to Allergy & Anaphylaxis Australia, egg allergy affects approximately 9% of Australian infants — making it the second most common food allergy in children under five. For diverse suburbs like Guildford, where family gatherings regularly cross dietary lines, an all-eggless menu serves the whole room without a second order.
