03 — Logo Usage
Logo Rules

Two approved logo files. Use them correctly. Never recreate, recolour, or distort them.

Primary — with name
Use on light cream or white backgrounds. Minimum width: 120px.
Icon only — transparent
Use on dark backgrounds, favicon, app icon, social avatar. Minimum: 64px.
✓ Do
  • Use on #FFF8F2 or white backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to the chef's hat height on all sides
  • Use icon-only version for small sizes (under 80px)
  • Use full logo on all primary marketing materials
✗ Don't
  • Don't place on busy photographs without overlay
  • Don't stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Don't recolour — the teal circle is fixed to the logo only
  • Don't use the teal logo colour as a brand colour elsewhere
04 — Brand Voice
How We Speak

Num Num's is warm but premium. Never loud. Never corporate. We celebrate real moments for real families.

Warm, not gushing
We're genuinely joyful — but we don't over-exclaim. No three exclamation marks. No ALL CAPS hype.
"Your cake, made exactly as you imagined it."
Premium, not pretentious
We're a local family business. We speak plainly. No French pastry-chef language. No unnecessary jargon.
"Fresh, eggless cakes — crafted for your occasion."
Community-aware
We serve South Asian, Muslim, and vegetarian families. Reference shared values — celebration, family, festivals — not demographics.
"Diwali deserves something special. We've got you."
Confident, not pushy
We don't beg for orders. We state what we offer clearly. The product sells itself. Our job is to make it easy to say yes.
"Order via WhatsApp. Ready in 48 hours."
Non-negotiables
→ Always mention 100% eggless in cake copy
→ Never mention delivery platforms for custom cakes
→ Never reference supplier names
→ Custom cakes: WhatsApp or phone only
→ Minimum 48hrs notice on every custom cake mention
→ "Adult Cakes" must never appear anywhere
