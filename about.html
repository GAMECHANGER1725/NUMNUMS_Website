Our Story

Where It All Began

Tarun Patel opened Num Num\'s Bakery on Wigram Street, Harris Park in 2019 with one rule: every cake we sell will be 100% eggless.

Harris Park is home to one of Sydney\'s largest South Asian communities. For the families who live here, vegetarian and Jain-friendly food is not a special request. It is just how people eat. Num Num\'s was built with that in mind from the start.

In December 2024, we opened our second store at Riverstone Shopping Centre, bringing the same cakes to Sydney\'s northwest.

Our Journey

A Timeline of Milestones

2019

Harris Park Opens

Tarun Patel opens the first Num Num's Bakery at 96/96 Wigram Street, Harris Park. 100% eggless from day one.

2020

Custom Cakes Take Off

Word spreads through the South Asian community. Custom wedding and birthday cakes become the bakery's signature offering.

2022

Online Ordering Expands

Num Num's joins Uber Eats, Menulog, and DoorDash — bringing ready-made sweets to all of Sydney.

2024

Riverstone Opens

Shop 8, Riverstone Shopping Centre opens in December 2024. Num Num\'s now serves both Harris Park and Sydney\'s northwest.
