## The non-negotiable: 100% eggless

"Every cake we sell will be 100% eggless. Not some cakes. Not most cakes. Every single one, every single time."

This is not a dietary niche — it is the bakery's entire identity and founding principle.

**Why it matters to customers:**
- Vegetarian families (including Hindu and Jain) can order any cake without checking or asking
- Jain-friendly (no eggs as standard)
- Halal-friendly (eggs excluded; no cross-contamination with non-halal products)
- Egg allergy sufferers can order safely
- No special requests needed — the default is inclusive

**Never frame eggless as a compromise.** It is a craft choice and a community commitment.

## What NOT to say in posts

- ❌ "We offer egg-free options" — wrong framing. Everything is eggless, not "an option"
- ❌ "Great for those with egg allergies" — too narrow; undersells the breadth of the audience
- ❌ "Similar to regular cakes" — defensive framing; never compare
- ❌ Claim delivery — pickup only
- ❌ "Same-day orders welcome" — 48-hour minimum is firm
- ❌ Generic suburb references ("across Sydney") — always use specific suburb names
