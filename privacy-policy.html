Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GNT Ventures Pty Ltd, trading as Num Num's Bakery ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal information in connection with the website numnumsbakery.com.au and our bakery operations at Harris Park and Riverstone, Sydney.

We are committed to complying with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide to us

When you place a cake order or enquire via WhatsApp or phone, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number (WhatsApp / mobile)
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Order details (cake type, size, flavour, pickup date, design brief)

We do not collect credit card details or payment information directly — payments are handled in-store at pick-up.

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical information through cookies and analytics tools, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address (anonymised)

2. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses the following third-party tools that set cookies:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — measures website traffic and user behaviour. Data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out via Google's opt-out browser add-on.
  • Google Tag Manager (GTM) — manages analytics and marketing tags on our behalf.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to browse this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfil your cake orders
  • Communicate with you about your order (via WhatsApp or phone)
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Improve our website and services through analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your personal information for unsolicited marketing without your consent.

4. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers — such as Google (analytics), solely for the purposes described above
  • Law enforcement — if required by law, court order, or government authority

5. Data Storage and Security

Order information communicated via WhatsApp is stored on WhatsApp's platform, subject to Meta's Privacy Policy. We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, and loss, and from unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

Our website is hosted on Netlify (United States). Your data may be processed outside of Australia, but is subject to appropriate privacy safeguards.

6. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Make a complaint if you believe we have breached the APPs

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related enquiries, corrections, or complaints, please contact:

  • Business: GNT Ventures Pty Ltd (Num Num's Bakery)
  • Email: info.numnumsbakery@gmail.com
  • Phone / WhatsApp: +61 425 697 725
  • Harris Park: 96/96 Wigram Street, Harris Park NSW 2150

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
