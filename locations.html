About Our Harris Park Store

Our original store at 96/96 Wigram Street sits right on the main strip of Harris Park, one of Sydney's most vibrant South Asian neighbourhoods, just a few minutes walk from Harris Park train station and less than 2 km from Parramatta CBD. The strip is well known for its authentic restaurants, grocers, and sweet shops, and Num Num's has been part of it since 2019.

We are open every day from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, making us one of the later-closing bakeries in the area, ideal if you need to pick up after work or following an evening out. Street parking is available on Wigram Street and the surrounding blocks. You will find us in the row of shops between the roundabout and the park.

When you visit, you can browse our display cakes, discuss a custom design in person, or simply pick up your pre-ordered cake. The team speaks English, Hindi, and Gujarati. Feel free to ask questions about any flavour or design. Phone: +61 425 697 725

About Our Riverstone Store

Our Riverstone store opened in December 2024 at Shop 8, Riverstone Shopping Centre, bringing Num Num's eggless cakes to Sydney's northwest for the first time. Riverstone Shopping Centre is a neighbourhood retail hub on Riverstone Parade, serviced by the Richmond bus routes and within easy reach of Riverstone train station on the Richmond line.

The Riverstone store keeps extended morning hours, opening from 6:00 AM Monday to Friday, making it the go-to stop for commuters and early risers in the Hills and Hawkesbury areas. Weekend hours are 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Free parking is available throughout the shopping centre car park directly outside.

Serving suburbs including Riverstone, Box Hill, Marsden Park, Schofields, Rouse Hill, and Vineyard, this store stocks the same full range of 15 eggless cake flavours and takes the same WhatsApp custom orders. Phone: +61 425 697 725
